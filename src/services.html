Services Details

Safe, reliable blood donation and supply services.

Share
Follow us:

At Unico Hospital PLC, we understand that having access to safe and screened blood is very important. That's why our advanced blood bank in Dhaka is open 24/7 to provide top-quality, reliable blood and blood component services to patients in need.

Our blood bank has the equipment, safety protocols, and modern facilities to meet the demand for various blood types. This is true whether there is an emergency, a patient is scheduled for surgery, or the patient has a chronic condition like thalassemia or cancer.

Who Needs Blood Transfusion?

Our blood bank supports patients undergoing:

  • Major surgeries (cardiac, orthopedic, etc.)
  • Accident and trauma cases
  • Cancer treatments
  • Pregnancy complications
  • Anemia and blood disorders like thalassemia or hemophilia

How to Donate Blood at Unico Hospital

We welcome healthy individuals to donate blood and save lives. To be eligible:

  • You must be aged 18–60
  • Weigh at least 50 kg
  • Be free from any major illness or infection

Our trained staff will guide you through a safe and comfortable donation process, including a brief health check-up.

Unico Hospital's Blood Bank is one of the most trusted facilities in Dhaka. It is known for its strong commitment to excellence, ethical standards, and patient care. At Unico Hospital, we make sure that every drop of blood donated or given as a transfusion is used wisely.
